Facility

Everything under one roof.

Cleaning, scanning, color, and restoration all happen in our Burbank facility. Your media never leaves the building or changes hands with an outside vendor.

Request a quote

Keeping every stage in one place is how we protect fragile originals: fewer hands, no shipping between vendors, and a controlled environment from the moment your media arrives until it leaves. Inspection, cleaning, scanning, color, and restoration all happen here, in Burbank.

The scanning floor
Scanning

Purpose-built scanners

Our patented scanners were developed specifically for fragile and deteriorated film, moving even severely shrunken stock gently, with minimal contact and nothing touching the image. The same floor handles everything from 8mm to 35mm, with HDR capture available for film that needs its full dynamic range recovered.

The color suites
Color & restoration

Graded and restored in-house

Color correction and restoration run on calibrated DaVinci Resolve suites in controlled lighting, so what we deliver is what you will actually see. Deeper restoration, repairing tears, stains, and missing frames, happens in the same building, on the same files.

Climate-controlled handling
Storage & handling

A controlled environment

Every original is inspected and triaged by hand on arrival, and film that needs it goes straight into cold storage. The handling and storage areas are temperature and humidity controlled, and your media is tracked from the moment it arrives until it ships back to you.
